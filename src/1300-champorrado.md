## Champorrado (Easy)

**INGREDIENTS**

- 1 cup rice
- 2 cups milk
- 2 cups water
- 1/3 to 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup cocoa powder

**INSTRUCTIONS**

1. mix all ingredients, and boil together
1. once boiling, mix frequenly
1. boil until the mixture develops a "skin". Turn off heat
1. let stand for 10 minutes
